## 﫧 Welcome to the Deep

**Club Siren** is an immersive underwater nightclub and social world inside VRChat.

It is a place where music, virtual nightlife, community, and ocean-inspired world design come together. Visitors can explore the reef, discover hidden spaces, meet new people, attend live events, and experience a nightclub atmosphere built beneath the sea.

Club Siren is created for:

* VRChat explorers
* Live DJs and performers
* Dancers and nightlife communities
* Virtual photographers and content creators
* World builders and digital creators
* Web3 and onchain communities
* Anyone ready to experience something below the surface

> No passport required—just enter VRChat and follow the current.
